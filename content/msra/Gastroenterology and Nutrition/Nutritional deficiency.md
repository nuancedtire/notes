- **Condition:** Scurvy  
- **Cause:** Deficiency of ascorbic acid (vitamin C)  
- **Symptoms:** 
  - Anaemia
  - Spongy gums
  - Mucocutaneous haemorrhages
  - General symptoms (irritability, anorexia)  
- **Common Age Group:** Infants aged 6 to 24 months
