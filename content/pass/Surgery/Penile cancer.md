Penile cancer is a rare form of cancer that is usually a squamous cell carcinoma.  
  
Features  
* penile lump
* penile ulceration

  
Risk factors:  
* Human immunodeficiency virus infection
* Human papillomavirus virus infection
* Genital warts
* Poor hygiene
* Phimosis
* Paraphimosis
* Balanitis
* Age \>50

  
Treatment:  
* Radiotherapy
* Chemotherapy
* Surgery

  
Prognosis:  
* Approximately 50% at 5 years
