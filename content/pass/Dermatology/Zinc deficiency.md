Features  
* acrodermatitis: red, crusted lesions
	+ acral distribution
	+ peri\-orificial
	+ perianal
* alopecia
* short stature
* hypogonadism
* hepatosplenomegaly
* geophagia (ingesting clay/soil)
* cognitive impairment

  
Acrodermatitis enteropathica is a recessively inherited partial defect in intestinal zinc absorption.
